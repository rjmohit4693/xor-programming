FAQ

  • How do I create my first sheet of music?

    • Simply click File > New, or press Ctrl + N, then complete the staff wizard.


  • How can I add multiple parts of the same instrument?

    • Add a new staff using the toolbar button, then select the same instrument.


  • How do I add or delete notes?

    • Click on the add note button or erase button in the toolbar, then click a valid place in a measure.


  • How do I change the length of a note?

    • Use the +,- keys on your keyboard or the scrollwheel.


  • How do I add or delete staves, measures, rests, or sharps?

    • Click on the corresponding buttons in the toolbar. See the toolbar help for a graphic.


  • How do I save my composition?

    • Click on the File menu tab and chose the save option.


  • How do I open/load my composition?

    • Click on the File menu tab and chose the open option.


  • How do I print my composition?

    • Click on the File menu tab and chose the print option.


  • How many instruments can I use in my composition?

    • You can use up to 15 instruments in your composition from the 128 instruments available.


  • How do I adjust the tempo?

    • You can adjust the tempo by using the BPM slider in the Sheet Wizard or the UI.


  • How do I hide/show the toolbar or status bar?

    • You can hide/show the toolbar or status bar by clicking on the Window menu tab and either checking or unchecking the appropriate box.
